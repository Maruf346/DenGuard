Our Mission

Denguard is committed to combating dengue outbreaks through intelligent forecasting and community-driven reporting. By leveraging AI and real-time data, our platform empowers citizens, doctors, and municipal bodies to stay ahead of potential outbreaks and take timely action.

What We Offer

  • AI-Based Prediction: Forecasts dengue outbreak risks using weather data and machine learning (SVM).
  • Doctor Reporting Dashboard: Health professionals can log real dengue cases and share expert advice.
  • Dynamic Heatmaps: Visualize high-risk zones based on confirmed cases and environmental conditions.
  • Action Recommendation System: Smart decision-making with Markov Decision Process (MDP) for municipal response planning.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Clean web interface for public awareness and real-time regional risk lookup.

Why Choose Denguard?

In regions where dengue outbreaks continue to threaten lives and resources, Denguard bridges the gap between data and decision-making. Our system combines real-time inputs with AI-based predictions to drive faster response, early warnings, and collaborative health defense.

How It Works

Denguard collects weather data such as rainfall, temperature, and humidity, along with manually entered dengue case reports from doctors. This information is processed using a Support Vector Machine (SVM) model to classify dengue outbreak risk in different regions. Based on the risk level, a Markov Decision Process (MDP) recommends smart municipal actions like fogging, awareness drives, or emergency alerts.

Data Sources

  • DGHS Bangladesh: Weekly dengue case reports
  • OpenWeatherMap API: Real-time and forecast weather data
  • Doctor Inputs: Verified case entries from hospital records
